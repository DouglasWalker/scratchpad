Bristol Rovers
VS.
Arsenal XI
1
Game Date: 18th July 2015, 3pm Kick Off.
Weather Conditions: 22° Overcast.
Referee: Douglas Walker
Attendance: 6720 (721 Away)
Gunner Stephy Mavididi proved the difference in this early pre-season friendly capitalising on an early defensive mixup between Tom Parkes & goalkeeper Steve Mildenhall. Rovers grew into the game in the second half and James Clarke shone in defence, but Rovers were unable to break the deadlock after never mounting any serious spells of sustained pressure on the young Arsenal XI's sides goal. Jake Gosling went close near the death, but Rovers fans will have been left encourages by the return to action of Jermaine Easter, who looked sharp after being brought on as a substitute on 60 minutes.
